Sun in Aries in 1st House

The Sun is perceived to be the king in the solar system as it holds all the celestial bodies around and dominates over them. For the same reasons, it will bestow same attributes to you and will make you stay strong from the core with a balanced composure of mind. You would be dominating and effective in appearance as you carry the strength of leaving marks behind. You are born leader but should stay away from overshadowing others.

The Sun in Aries provides wisdom and true knowledge to you. You would carry a positive attitude towards life along with the firmness in approach as you would not let others affect your approach. You are a mature person but are very much concerned about your presence. You are vigorous and courageous which makes you exploring in nature but this could also turn you snobbish at times. You carry much self belief which will take you towards good heights if it wont become over confidence. The positive impacts of Sun would endow you with clear vision towards life.

You are usually gifted with a direct approach towards life and are always one step ahead of the crowd. You like to take charge of a situation and lead other people. You are usually certain about situations and born leaders. You usually have a jolly nature and can brighten the mood of others. You are humorous and charismatic. You crave excitement and often feel restless because of such traits.

You can sometimes take unnecessary risks and may rush into situations without taking into account all the crucial details. You often learn a lot through experience. You have a mind that filters out the non-essentials and this clear sight helps you make speedy progress in life. You keep your mind fixed towards a goal and work to achieve it.

On the other hand, you could also turn aggressive and stubborn along with a tough heart but you are not wicked souls as you know the difference between evil and pure and do believe upon moral path. You would attain strong position through your intellect and hard work besides which you are well suited for political career. In the end, you could have eye problems and should hold upon your much risk taking tendency.
